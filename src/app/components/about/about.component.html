About us:



The project that has been developed consists of two parts, a device and an app connected to it.

** The device makes the readings of the amperage consumed in the electrical devices that are wanted through sensors.

** The mentioned readings will be stored in a database for your later study.

** The mobile application will collect said data to show them to the users on demand and will report the consumption and the real-time cost of the devices connected to the device mentioned above.
